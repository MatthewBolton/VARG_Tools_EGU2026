EGU 2026 conference demo

This conference build accompanies the EGU 2026 poster "VARG-Tools: Browser-Based Software to Streamline Tephra Correlation and Bayesian Age Modelling". Launch the hosted demo in your browser or open the meeting abstract for the poster context.

VARG-Tools supports geochemical data processing, visualization, and chronology building for tephra correlation workflows. This page is tailored for conference visitors who want to try the software after seeing the poster and abstract.
